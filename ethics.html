IT Ethics

Guidelines for Developers

Developers must follow ethical principles such as ensuring user privacy, building accessible applications, and minimizing harm. For more details, refer to the ACM Code of Ethics.

Data Scientists and Ethical Considerations

Data scientists should be transparent about the algorithms they use and consider the societal impact of their work. A great reference is the Data Science Ethics website.
